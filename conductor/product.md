# Initial Concept

A web app designed to manage a family’s finances. It should allow users to easily add purchases, expenses, and income. The app should provide reports for the family. Each family can create its own custom categories. Each family should be able to register the bank accounts they use. Everything should be easy to access and set up. When recording expenses, users should have a clean and simplified interface. Each user can have their own account and create a family code. If another user joins that family, they are automatically linked to the family and will share full access. There should also be a feature to track savings and investments.

# Product Guide: Family Finance Web App

## Product Vision
A collaborative web application designed to empower families and couples to manage their shared finances with ease and transparency. The app focuses on simplifying the tracking of income, expenses, and savings while providing clear, visual insights into financial health.

## Target Users
- **Primary:** Couples managing joint bank accounts and shared household expenses.
- **Secondary:** Families seeking to involve multiple members in shared budgeting and savings goals.

## Strategic Goals
- **Financial Transparency:** Provide a clear, unified view of all income and expenses across shared accounts.
- **Saving/Investment Tracking:** Enable users to set savings goals and track their progress towards financial security.
- **Collaborative Management:** Facilitate seamless contribution and communication between family members regarding their shared budget.

## Core Features (MVP)
- **Family Collaboration:** Simple account creation with a "family code" system to link users to a shared group.
- **Bank Account Management:** Registry for multiple bank accounts used by the family.
- **Transaction Tracking:** Clean, simplified interface for recording income, purchases, and expenses.
- **Custom Categories:** Ability for each family to define and manage their own expense/income categories.
- **Visual Reports:** Intuitive charts and graphs to visualize spending patterns and progress towards savings goals.
- **Savings & Investment Tracking:** Dedicated feature to monitor savings and investment performance.

## User Experience Principles
- **Web-First (Responsive):** Accessible across all devices via a modern browser, ensuring accessibility at home or on the go.
- **Clean & Simplified:** Focus on ease of use and speed for daily transaction entry.
- **Secure & Private:** Protecting sensitive financial information while enabling shared access.
